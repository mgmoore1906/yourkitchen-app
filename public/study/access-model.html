YourKitchen · The Access Model

One kitchen. Three ways to fund it.

Households, care organizations, and enterprise employers look like three different products. Underneath, they’re the same one. The model diverges at the top — who sponsors and how it’s funded — and converges on a single, identical kitchen.

Who sponsors & funds it
Household
Care+ · you pay
  • Up to 3 kitchens, addresses independent
  • Light OTP login, scoped per kitchen
  • Sponsor creates anytime
  • Caring across distance
Organization pool
Org pays · seats
  • Pool of seats, time-boxed & recycled
  • Full recipient account per person
  • Org sets the activation window
  • Hospice · home health · MCO
Enterprise
Employer pays
  • SSO eligibility, separate logins
  • Self-serve anytime, or event-triggered
  • Employee becomes their own sponsor
  • HR benefited · total rewards · EAP

Build the kitchen once.

Organization pool and enterprise are layers on top of the same primitives — not separate products, not rebuilds. Consumer revenue funds the B2B motion, and B2B falls out of the consumer build. The kitchen never gets built twice.
