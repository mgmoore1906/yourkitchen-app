When life gets heavy, the people who love you can make sure you eat.

YourKitchen lets a circle of friends and family send real, home-delivered restaurant meals to someone going through a hard season — a new baby, an illness, a loss. They claim a date, you confirm with a text, and dinner shows up. No one has to ask “what can I do?” again.

Care
For anyone gathering meals around someone they love.
What’s included
  • A kitchen for your recipient
  • Unlimited calendar — claim any date
  • Add favorite restaurants & meals
  • Home delivery by courier
  • Y/N text confirmation before any charge
  • Live delivery tracking for the recipient
  • Delivery confirmation text to the sender
  • Shareable kitchen link & invites
  • Village board — notes from supporters
  • Insights — meals received, support shown
