When life gets heavy, the people who love you can make sure you eat.

YourKitchen lets a circle of friends and family send real, home-delivered restaurant meals to someone going through a hard season — a new baby, an illness, a loss. They claim a date, you confirm with a text, and dinner shows up. No one has to ask “what can I do?” again.
